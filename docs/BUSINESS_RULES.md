# Gilded Rose — Inventory Rules

How our stock changes in value as time passes. These are the rules the inn lives by when updating inventory each day.

---

## The basics

Every item has:

- **Days left to sell** — how many days we have before the sell-by date  
- **Quality** — how valuable the item is to customers  

At the end of each day, both usually change.

**Quality never goes below zero**, and for almost everything **it never goes above 50**.

---

## Ordinary goods

Most products (vests, elixirs, and so on) lose value as they get closer to their sell-by date.

- Each day, quality drops a little.  
- **After** the sell-by date has passed, they lose value **twice as quickly**.

---

## Aged Brie

Cheese that gets better with age.

- Quality **increases** every day.  
- After the sell-by date, it improves **even faster**.  
- Still never worth more than **50**.

---

## Backstage passes

Tickets to the TAFKAL80ETC concert.

- The closer the concert, the more valuable the pass becomes.  
- When there are **10 days or fewer** left, value rises faster.  
- When there are **5 days or fewer** left, value rises faster still.  
- **After the concert**, the pass is worthless — quality drops to **zero**.

---

## Sulfuras

A legendary item. We do not treat it like normal stock.

- It **never** needs to be sold by a date.  
- Its quality **never changes**.  
- It stays at legendary quality (**80**).

---

## Conjured items

Goods from our new conjured supplier (for example, Conjured Mana Cake).

- They follow the same idea as ordinary goods — they lose value over time, and faster after the sell-by date.  
- The difference: they degrade **twice as fast** as ordinary goods.

---

## At a glance

| Item type | What happens to quality over time |
|-----------|-----------------------------------|
| Ordinary goods | Steadily loses value; loses value faster after sell-by |
| Conjured items | Same as ordinary, but twice as fast |
| Aged Brie | Gains value as it ages |
| Backstage passes | Gains value as the concert nears; worthless afterwards |
| Sulfuras | Never changes |
